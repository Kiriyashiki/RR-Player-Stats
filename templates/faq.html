How frequently is the leaderboard updated?

The database obtains fresh data every minute.

Why is some data missing? / Why is my VR not being updated?

This can happen if the database or RWFC's servers fail. You can check "Last update" in the leaderboard page to know when was the data last updated. Additionally, you need to stay online (in a public room) for at least a few minutes to be seen on the data.

Why am I/is a player banned?

This means the player has flagged the anti-cheat measures of the leaderboard against VR cheating, or was banned by a moderator for cheating VR. This does not mean the player is banned from RWFC! If you believe you were wrongly banned, please reach out in Discord (username in the footer at the bottom of the page)
